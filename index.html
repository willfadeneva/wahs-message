Thank You for 15 Years with WAHS

For fifteen years, iwahs.org has had the privilege of supporting WAHS as a trusted home for Hallyu research, scholarly exchange, and global conversation on Korean popular culture.

We are deeply grateful to the researchers, scholars, students, educators, media practitioners, branch partners, and community members who have been part of this journey. Your continued support has helped shape the World Association for Hallyu Studies into the global academic community it is today.

As we look toward the future, our commitment remains unchanged: to bridge rigorous multidisciplinary research with practical insight into the Korean Wave and its international cultural impact.

WAHS is still here, and we look forward to welcoming you again soon.
